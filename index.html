Birthday cake

A birthday cake is a cake eaten as part of a birthday celebration. Birthday cakes are often layer cakes with frosting served with small lit candles on top representing the celebrant's age. Variations include cupcakes, cake pops, pastries, and tarts.

Anniversary Cake

For some newlyweds, celebrating their first anniversary means eating a helping of their preserved wedding cake together. The cake-freezing ritual stems from a 19th-century convention in which partners saved the top tier for their first child's christening (with some believing it to be an omen of good luck).

Wedding Cake

In modern Western culture, the cake is usually on display and served to guests at the reception. Traditionally , wedding cakes were made to bring good luck to all guests and the couple.
